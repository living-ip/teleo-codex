## Content

Stani Kulechov, founder and CEO of Aave (the largest DeFi lending protocol, $40B+ TVL, $1T+ originated loans), publishes a long-form article arguing that DAOs must evolve from bureaucratic token-voting governance toward founder-led execution with onchain accountability.

### Key Arguments

**DeFi's scale problem:**
- Aave holds ~30% of DeFi's total TVL — but DeFi TVL (~$120B) hasn't grown much since Aug 2021
- Global lending market is tens of trillions; US mortgage lending alone is ~$12T
- Market dominance within DeFi is not a moat — well-funded competitors can move faster

**DAOs as currently run are dysfunctional:**
- "Extraordinarily difficult, and not in the way that building hard things is difficult. They're difficult in the way of fighting your own organizational structure every single day."
- Proposals that should take a day take weeks of forum posts, temperature checks, and multiple votes
- DAOs become politicized quickly — voting becomes about attention, participants form political alliances
- "Just as large companies end up with managers instead of founders, DAOs end up with politicians"
- "It can often feel like we took the worst parts of corporate bureaucracy and removed the parts that create accountability in the name of decentralization"

**The proposed evolution:**
- Keep what DAOs got right: rules in code, transparent treasury, token holder safeguards on major decisions
- Founders and teams must lead execution — "Someone needs to wake up every morning with the full context in their head and make hard calls"
- Key differentiator: decisions and performance are all onchain and transparent; token holders can fire the team when objectives aren't met
- "Accountability is verifiable, and that is what separates this from a traditional company. There is no vendor lock-in."
- Governance should focus on collective input items (major protocol changes, treasury strategy); everything else is execution

**Aave's strategic moves:**
- Retired products under Avara umbrella to redirect all talent to Aave
- Lens was acquired by ecosystem company; Family wallet and Avara brand sunsetted
- Focused product layer: Aave App, Aave Pro, Aave Kit (full-stack DeFi)
- V4 = modular lending infrastructure for all use cases
- "Aave Will Win" proposal: becoming the most trusted finance brand
- Revenue from both protocol and product layers, vertically and horizontally
- Mission: "rearchitect all the world's lending infrastructure"

**Notable context:**
- Survived 4 years of SEC investigation
- Aave V3 secures $40B+ in assets
- Stani frames this as still "the very beginning of the Aave story"
