# ORE: Launch a boost for HNT-ORE?

## Summary
Proposal to introduce a liquidity boost for the HNT-ORE trading pair with the same multiplier value as ORE-ISC, and formalize a 3-tier boost multiplier system: vanilla ORE stake (tier 1), critical pairs like SOL-ORE and USDC-ORE (tier 2), and extended pairs like ISC-ORE and HNT-ORE (tier 3). The boost applies to kTokens representing shares in Kamino vaults managing concentrated liquidity positions on Orca.

## Significance
This proposal demonstrates futarchy being used for operational DeFi protocol governance rather than just fundraising. The formalization of a 3-tier boost system creates a governance framework where future multiplier changes apply to entire tiers rather than individual pairs, simplifying community proposals. The strategic focus on HNT (Helium Network Token) following HIP-138's tokenomics consolidation shows ORE positioning itself as a liquidity hub for real-world asset tokens and DePIN credits on Solana.
