# Futarchy Arena

Futarchy Arena was a competitive onchain futarchy game where players predicted outcomes of strategic decisions instead of voting. The project attempted to gamify futarchy governance through seasonal leaderboards, performance tracking, and measurable decision outcomes. The fundraise launched on Futardio on 2026-03-04 with a $50,000 target but failed to reach its goal, closing in refunding status with only $934 committed.
