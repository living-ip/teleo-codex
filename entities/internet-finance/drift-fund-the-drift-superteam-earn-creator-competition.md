# Drift: Fund The Drift Superteam Earn Creator Competition

## Summary
Proposal to fund a creator competition with $8,250 in prizes distributed across three content tracks (video, Twitter threads, trade ideas) plus a grand prize, aimed at promoting B.E.T (Solana's first capital efficient prediction market built on Drift) through community engagement and content generation via Superteam Earn platform.

## Significance
Represents an early futarchy-governed marketing spend decision for a DeFi protocol launch. The proposal tested whether prediction markets could effectively evaluate community engagement initiatives and marketing ROI. The failure suggests either insufficient market participation, skepticism about the competition's value proposition, or concerns about token dilution for marketing purposes.
