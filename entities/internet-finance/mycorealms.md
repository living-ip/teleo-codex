# MycoRealms

MycoRealms is the first futarchy-governed agricultural operation, raising $125,000 USDC on Futardio to build and operate a mushroom production facility. The project applies MetaDAO's conditional market governance to physical infrastructure, where all capital expenditures beyond a $10,000 monthly allowance require market approval. The team cannot access the treasury directly — the first post-raise proposal will be a $50,000 CAPEX withdrawal for infrastructure (accommodation, 3 growing rooms, DG set), which must pass through decision markets before funds deploy.

The project targets button mushroom production initially (fast crop cycles, standardized production), scaling to 12 growing rooms, then medicinal mushrooms and export. All invoices, expenses, harvest records, and operational photos publish to Arweave for full transparency. Team: crypticmeta (Solana/Bitcoin dev, built OrdinalNovus to $30M volume) and Ram (5+ years commercial mushroom production).

## Timeline
- **2025** — Team preparation: internship at ICAR-DMR Solan (India's national mushroom research institute), hands-on work in commercial farms, market research, vendor quotations, verbal commitments from 15+ wholesalers, Detailed Project Report aligned with ICAR economic models
